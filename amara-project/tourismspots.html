Favourite Tourist Spots

Murree


Paradise
Murree, once a tourist paradise for many Pakistani families is now losing its aesthetic appeal and the star-dust winged air.
Traffic Mess
Murree is renowned to be a traffic mess. This is because of its ill managed traffic and parking system. It only has a parking capacity of 3,500 vehicles
Alternative Exits
Murree also lacks alternative exits, because of which thousands of cars are funneled to a specific point on main mall and Murree road causing problems

Naran Valley


People Dressing
The first sight that caught my attention were the people. Dressed modestly in clothes that were far from fancy, these people on the roads seemed to be filled with wonder.
Taxila
We soon passed through the city of Taxila. The road sides of the entire city were loitered (in the literal sense) with tomb stones of every kind imaginable.
Haripur
We passed through the beautiful village of Haripur and Havailian and I was fascinated by the beautiful structures that were homes to so many people,
